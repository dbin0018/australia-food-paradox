Australia's
Food Paradox

Australians have never spent more on fast food.. and never spent more trying to undo it

FIT2179 Data Visualisation 2  ·  Dayana Syahrizah Binti Zohari  ·  May 2026

Section 01

We love our fast food

Australia's fast food industry has grown every year for the past decade. From suburban drive-throughs to CBD lunch spots, takeaway culture is deeply embedded in how Australians eat.

Section 02

Then delivery made it even easier

When UberEats and DoorDash arrived, fast food didn't just stay convenient, it became frictionless. Australians embraced online ordering rapidly.

Section 03

But we're also "getting healthy"

At the same time, spending on health food stores, protein supplements, and weight loss programs has surged.

Section 04

The paradox, side by side

Australians are aware they eat unhealthily, and yet the fast food industry keeps growing.
